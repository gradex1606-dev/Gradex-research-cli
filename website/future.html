Where We're Going

From agent-assisted workflows to fully autonomous engineering systems.

Today

Agent-assisted engineering

Developers run parallel AI agents with benchmarks and gates. Every experiment is measured against a baseline before anything merges.

Tomorrow

Agent-driven engineering

Agents propose, test, and refine improvements continuously. Teams review evidence-backed results instead of debating untested ideas.

Future

Autonomous engineering systems

Self-directed experimentation across repositories, intelligent rollbacks, and adaptive benchmarks that keep software improving on its own.
